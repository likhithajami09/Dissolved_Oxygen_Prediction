PROBLEM STATEMENT

Challenges in Dissolved Oxygen Prediction

Traditional methods encounter difficulties in accurately predicting dissolved oxygen levels in aquaculture due to its nonlinear and dynamic nature.

Hybrid Model Approach

The study introduces a hybrid model, LightGBM-BiSRU, combining linear interpolation, the LightGBM algorithm, and attention mechanisms to address these challenges.

Enhanced Accuracy and Efficiency

The aim of the hybrid model is to improve prediction accuracy and efficiency, crucial for effectively regulating water quality in intensive aquaculture operations.
